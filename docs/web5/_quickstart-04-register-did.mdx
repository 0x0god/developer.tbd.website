## 4. Register your DID:

Registering your DID gives others a way to find you, message you, and send you data (provided they have permissions).

```js
await web5.did.register({
    connected: true,
    did: did.id,
    endpoint: 'app://dwn',
    keys: did.keys[0].keypair,
});
```
